- [ ] What are Mandelbrot, Julia, Burningship, Tricorn sets?
  - 수식 정리하기.
  - Mandelbrot: $z_n+1 = z_n ^2 + c$
  - Julia: $z_n+1 = z_n ^2 + c$
  - Burningship: $z_n+1 = (|Re(z_n)| + |Im(z_n)|i)^2 + c$
  - Tricorn: $z_n+1 = (z_n)* ^2 + c$
